The grand duchy operates as a semi-constitutional monarchy, in which the national constitution explicitly vests political power and governing authority in the reigning grand duke while simultaneously defining the limites of those powers. The current monarch, Grand Duke Alexander, fulfills the various constitutional responsibilities of The Crown "in consultation with" the Council of State, which is appointed by the grand duke and functions as an executive cabinet, a royal privy council, and an *ad hoc* judicial tribunal. In effect, the legislative functions of the government are carried out by The Sovereign together with the Council of State, acting as the Crown-in-Council.

As a unitary state, Eniarku's three territorial subdivisions (known as "cantons") do not currently possess any devolved governmental powers of their own and exist primarily for administrative purposes. While the constitution does not prohibit the government from granting the cantons some degree of autonomy, the are currently managed directly by the grand duke and the Council of State from the capital in Glenora.
