CHAPTER I
SOVEREIGNTY

Article 1.—The Grand Duchy of Eniarku is a sovereign, independent, and secular State within the frameworks of international and intermicronational law.

Article 2.—The Grand Duchy of Eniarku is the sole successor State to the Kingdom of Eniarku and any prior or present incarnations thereof.

Article 3.—The sovereignty and territory of the Grand Duchy of Eniarku are inviolable.
