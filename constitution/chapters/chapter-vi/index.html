CHAPTER VI
THE PUBLIC WELFARE

Article 32.—The Armed Forces serve as the principal military, intelligence, security, and police unit of the State.

Article 33.—The Sovereign serves as the head of the Armed Forces.

Article 34.—The regulation of the Armed Forces is to be provided for by law.
