CHAPTER IX
FINAL PROVISIONS

Article 42.—Any and all prior constitutional provisions for the State of Eniarku are hereby repealed.

Article 43.—Any and all other prior laws and regulations of the State of Eniarku currently in force are hereby repealed.

Article 44.—Neither this Constitution, nor any of its individual provisions, may be suspended.

Article 45.—This Constitution enters into force at 12:00AM EST on 29 May 2024.
