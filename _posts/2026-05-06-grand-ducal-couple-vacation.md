**BALDWIN HOUSE, Glenora** — TRH Grand Duke Alexander and Grand Duchess Elizabeth returned from their belated honeymoon earlier this evening, concluding their first royal trip abroad as husband and wife. The grand ducal couple spent a week in the Dominican Republic after departing the grand duchy last Wednesday, at which time HRH The Prince of August was temporarily deputized as the Lieutenant of the Grand Duchy under Article 19 of the constitution.

While the next session of the Council of State has not yet been announced by Baldwin House, councillors are expected to convene at least once before the Jubilee begins on Monday, May 25, which coincides with Memorial Day in the United States.
