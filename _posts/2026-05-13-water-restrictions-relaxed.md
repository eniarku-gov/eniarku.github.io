title:  "Water restrictions eased for Glenora and Palma"
date:   2026-05-13 14:00:00 -0500
categories: press
---

**BALDWIN HOUSE, Glenora** — Following a joint announcement by the local utility authority this afternoon that pressure has been restored to the repaired 42-inch water main, the usage restrictions announced early Sunday morning have been modified. Residents in both Glenora and Palma may resume using water in normal amounts, but should continue to boil all water meant for consumption until further notice. Full testing of the water flowing through the repaired supply line is anticipated to be completed within the next two weeks.
