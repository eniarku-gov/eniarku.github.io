Section 9.002 — Definitions.

As used in this act:

(a) "Adult" means a person who is 18 years of age or older.

(b) "Alien" means a person who is a foreign national or a foreign citizen that does not hold Eniarkian citizenship.

(c) "Citizen" means a person to whom the government has granted the full rights, obligations, and protections due to a member of a state, whether at birth, by naturalization, or by option.

(d) "Minor" means a person who is less than 18 years of age.

(e) "National" means a person who, by birth, naturalization, or another process, owes permanent allegiance to a state.
