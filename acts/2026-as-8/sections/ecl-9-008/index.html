Section 9.008 — Citizenship; renunciation; requirements; process; provide for.

(1) A citizen that satisfies all of the following conditions may, by the process prescribed in subsection (2), apply to renounce their citizenship:

(a) The citizen is an adult.

(b) The citizen is not the subject of a pending or ongoing legal proceeding in Eniarku.

(c) The citizen is of sound mind and is not subject to duress or external coercion.

(d) The citizen would not be rendered stateless by renouncing their Eniarkian citizenship.

(2) A citizen may renounce their citizenship at any time by submitting a declaration in a form prescribed by the Lord High Chancellor The declaration must include, but is not limited to, an attestation of all of the following:

(a) The declarant is an adult.

(b) The declarant is not the subject of a pending or ongoing legal proceeding in Eniarku.

(c) The declarant is of sound mind and is not subject to duress or external coercion.

(d) The declarant will not be rendered stateless by renouncing their Eniarkian citizenship.

(e) The declarant no longer wishes to remain an Eniarkian citizen.

(f) The declarant understands that, upon approval of the declaration, they will no longer be entitled to the rights, obligations, and protections due to a member of a state.

(3) The Lord High Chancellor shall review a declaration submitted under subsection (2) with all deliberate speed and shall grant the renunciation of citizenship request to the declarant subject to subsection (4) and any other applicable law.

(4) The Lord High Chancellor shall deny a declaration submitted under subsection (2) if, upon thorough review of the declaration, the Lord High Chancellor determines, based on clear and convincing evidence, the declarant knowingly made a false attestation of any item required under subsection (2).

(5) A person who renounces their citizenship under this section shall be ineligible to reapply for citizenship for a period of not less than 1 year.
