Section 8.004 — Lord High Treasurer; create; powers and duties.

(1) The Lord High Treasurer shall be appointed by and serve at the pleasure of The Sovereign as the head of the Crown Treasury.

(2) The Lord High Treasurer shall serve as the implementing agent of all laws relating to the Crown Treasury and provide regular reports to the Council of State on the activities of the Crown Treasury.
