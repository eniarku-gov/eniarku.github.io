Section 9.1104 — Domestic abuse.

"Domestic abuse" means an act or recent act by a current or former cohabitant, spouse, or dating, romantic, or sexual partner against a person similarly situated or a child thereof that results in or is reasonably likely to result in death, serious bodily or mental harm, or sexual abuse or exploitation.
