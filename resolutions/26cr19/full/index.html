26CR19

A resolution for an ACT OF STATE to ratify the treaties, conventions, and protocols of the Conference of Santiago; and to provide for the powers and duties of the government thereof.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE CROWN-IN-COUNCIL:

Sec. 10.101. This act may be cited as the "Conference of Santiago Ratification Act".

Sec. 10.102. The Council of State hereby advises and consents to the ratification of the following treaties, conventions, and protocols, all such documents being integral components of the Convention of Santiago:

(a) The Treaty of Santiago.

(b) The Treaty of Persenburg.

(c) The Protocol of Goetha.

(d) The Convention of Mauritsstad.

(e) The Convention of Oswiencin.

Sec. 10.103. As soon as practicable after the effective date of this act, the Council of State shall, by resolution, propose acts of state, orders-in-council, or both to implement the requirements of the treaties, conventions, and protocols listed under ECL 10.302. The Council of State shall adopt a resolution proposed under this section within 30 days.
