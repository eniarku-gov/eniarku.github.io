24CR1

A resolution for certain ORDERS-IN-COUNCIL on the orders of chivalry of the state.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE CROWN-IN-COUNCIL:

It is this day ordered by His Royal Highness in Council that the Most Noble Order of The Crown, the Military Order of The Cossacks, and the Order of Eniarku, established by the the Honours and Titles Act of 2024, and the Royal Alexandrian Order, established by His Royal Highness's Letters Patent of the 5th day of August 2024, are hereby and henceforth to be designated as orders of chivalry.

It is further ordered that the Meritorious Service Cross and the Royal Citation, established by the Honours and Titles Act of 2024, are hereby and henceforth to be designated as decorations of state.

It is further ordered that the Order of the Peony, established by the Law of the Grand Ducal House, is hereby and henceforth to be designated as a royal house order.
