Eniarku is an autonomous micronation claiming sovereignty over territory in continental North America. Originally created as a small-scale political experiment, Eniarku declared its independence from the United States of America on 29 May 2009. Shortly after its founding, the young republic joined the MicroWiki community and began developing relationships with other micronations, may of which persist to the present day. Over more than a decade, Eniarku has evolved into a sophisticated state with its own culture and customs while remaining grounded in the Enlightenment era ideals of reason, liberty, and natural rights espoused by its founders.
