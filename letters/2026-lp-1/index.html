ALEXANDER THE FIRST by the Grace and Will of the People of the Grand Duchy of Eniarku and of Our Other Realms and Territories Sovereign Grand Duke to all Princes Dukes Marquesses Counts Barons Knights The Lords and Councillors Assembled and all other Our faithful Subjects and Citizens whatsoever to whom these Presents shall come, Greeting.

WHEREAS We are desirous to preserve the status of Our Royal Alexandrian Order in the Order of Precedence of the various Orders of Chivalry of the State and to maintain consistency in the grades and ranks of the Order relative to others in the Order of Precedence.

NOW KNOW YE that We of Our especial grace, certain knowledge, and mere motion do by these Presents ordain that the Order of Chivalry constituted as The Royal Alexandrian Order shall continue to rank immediately before The Most Honourable Order of the Cossacks and immediately after The Most Noble Order of The Crown;

AND, that it is ordained that this Order shall henceforth consist of The Sovereign and a single Class, namely Knight Commanders and Dame Commanders;

AND, that it is ordained that any member of this Order may, on all occasions when the use of such letters is customary, place after their name the appropriate letters, namely KCA or DCA, as appropriate;

AND, that those other Presents embodied in Our Letters Patent numbered 2024-1, dated the fifth day of August 2024, and the Statutes and Ordinances of this Order shall continue to remain in force.

BY WARRANT UNDER THE GRAND DUKE'S SIGN MANUAL

Alexander

25 May MMXXVI
