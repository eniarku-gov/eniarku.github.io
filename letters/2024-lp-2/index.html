ALEXANDER THE FIRST by the Grace and Will of the People of the Grand Duchy of Eniarku and of Our Other Realms and Territories Sovereign Grand Duke to all Princes Dukes Marquesses Counts Barons Knights The Lords and Councillors Assembled and all other Our faithful Subjects and Citizens whatsoever to whom these Presents shall come, Greeting.

WHEREAS We are sensible of the high regard and affection in which members of Our Partner’s family are held by Our loving subjects, We are desirous of creating them as members of the House of Abbott and conferring upon them the Styles and Honours of the same.

NOW KNOW YE that We of Our especial grace, certain knowledge, and mere motion do by these Presents create the parents, siblings, and descendants thereof of The Right Honourable Lady Elizabeth, Countess of Fairview, as members of the House of Abbott and confer upon them the Styles, Honours, and Dignities of the same to have and at all times to hold and enjoy, in addition to any other Titles of Honour which shall to them belong or at any time hereafter may belong.

BY WARRANT UNDER THE GRAND DUKE'S SIGN MANUAL

Alexander

6 September MMXXIV
