ALEXANDER THE FIRST by the Grace and Will of the People of the Grand Duchy of Eniarku and of Our Other Realms and Territories Sovereign Grand Duke to all Princes Dukes Marquesses Counts Barons Knights The Lords and Councillors Assembled and all other Our faithful Subjects and Citizens whatsoever to whom these Presents shall come, Greeting.

WHEREAS We are sensible of Our friendship with His Imperial and Royal Apostolic Majesty Oscar, Emperor-King of Karnia-Ruthenia, the high regard in which he is held by Our partners in the Community, and his personal and diplomatic contributions to Our Nation’s prosperity, We are desirous of creating him as a member of the various Orders of Our State and conferring upon him the Styles and Honours of the same.

NOW KNOW YE that We of Our especial grace, certain knowledge, and mere motion do by these Presents create

OSCAR, EMPEROR-KING OF KARNIA-RUTHENIA, as Knight Companion of The Most Noble Order of The Crown (KOC); as Commander of The Royal Alexandrian Order (CAO); and as Member of The Order of Eniarku (OE)–

and confer upon him the Styles, Honours, and Dignities of the same to have and at all times to hold and enjoy, in addition to any other Titles of Honour which shall to him belong or at any time hereafter may belong.

BY WARRANT UNDER THE GRAND DUKE'S SIGN MANUAL

Alexander

13 September MMXXIV
